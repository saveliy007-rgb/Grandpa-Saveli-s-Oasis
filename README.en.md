## How it works

1. **Turbine** spins in the wind — pulls in air.
2. **Cyclone** cleans dust and sand from the airflow.
3. **Pipe-in-pipe** delivers air 3–5 meters underground.
4. **Stainless steel** removes the electrostatic charge from water molecules → vapor turns into droplets.
5. **Earth (+8°C)** absorbs excess heat (phase transition).
6. **Geotextile wick** raises distilled water to the roots (like a kerosene lamp).

The water is distilled — a blank slate.  
You can add minerals to the wick — the tree gets fed.

---

## Why this matters

Every tree its own well.  
A desert no longer has to be a desert.

## Safety

You can add a radio beacon powered by a supercapacitor.  
If the turbine stops, the beacon transmits: "Repair needed."
